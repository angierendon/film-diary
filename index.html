My Film Diary

35mm · Life on Camera

Brooklyn Spring 2021

When I moved to NYC for college in 2021, it was a diffuclt and lonely time because of COVID. Walking around and taking photos helped my mental health. I took to the streets with a cheap point-and-shoot that I bought on ebay. Not great quality, but I was discovering my love for photography here!

KODAK PORTRA 400 01 ISO 400 ▸ 35mm
Los Angeles Summer 2023

During college, I bought a more sophisticated camera and started to capture higher quality images. I was lucky enough to make an amazing friend group, and we got to take a trip together. I finally felt happy and well-adjusted.

ILFORD HP5 02 ISO 400 ▸ 35mm
Miami Summer 2024

After I graduated college, I went back home for a while. I was really bored and lonely in my suburban Florida neighborhood; I missed my friends and the excitment of living in New York. But I used photography to find joy in the small things around me, and it helped me appreciate this transition period more.

FUJI SUPERIA 400 03 ISO 400 ▸ 35mm
Upper East Side Winter 2026

Now, I'm in grad school, back in New York City, and living in the cutest apartment with my best friends from college. I live 15 minutes away from Central Park, where I played in the snow and took photos after the snowstorm in January. I don't know what life will look like after graduating, but right now I'm happy.
